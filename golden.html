The Harmandir Sahib

The Harmandir Sahib, also known as the Golden Temple, is the holiest Gurdwara and the most important pilgrimage site of Sikhism. It is located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India. The temple is renowned for its serene atmosphere and stunning gold-plated structure, which is surrounded by a large lake known as the Amrit Sarovar.
