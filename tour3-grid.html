Andhra Pradesh

Tirupati

Tirupati: Famous for the Sri Venkateswara Temple, one of the richest and most visited Hindu temples in the world, located atop the Tirumala Hills.

Divine Pilgrimage: Exploring the Sacred City of Tirupati
  • Spiritual Hub: Immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance of Tirupati, one of the holiest cities in Hinduism, renowned for the Sri Venkateswara Temple atop the Tirumala Hills.
  • Sri Venkateswara Temple: Visit the iconic Sri Venkateswara Temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (a form of Lord Vishnu), and witness the grandeur of its architecture and religious rituals
  • Tirumala Hills: Ascend the scenic Tirumala Hills by road or foot, offering panoramic views of the surrounding countryside and the temple town of Tirupati below.


Andhra Pradesh

Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam (Vizag): Known for its beaches like Ramakrishna Beach and Rushikonda Beach, as well as the INS Kursura Submarine Museum and Kailasagiri Hill Park.

Coastal Delights: Unwinding in Vishakhapatnam's Scenic Beauty
  • Coastal Charms: Discover the coastal beauty of Vishakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, with its pristine beaches, scenic hills, and cultural attractions.
  • RK Beach: Relax on the golden sands of RK Beach (Ramakrishna Beach), one of the most popular beaches in Vizag, offering stunning sunsets and recreational activities.
  • Kailasagiri Hill: Ascend Kailasagiri Hill for breathtaking views of Vizag city and its coastline, and explore the Kailasagiri Park with its scenic viewpoints, toy train, and giant statue of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati.


Andhra Pradesh

Araku Valley

Araku Valley: The proposed capital city known for the Amaravati Stupa, an ancient Buddhist monument, as well as the Amareswara Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and the Undavalli Caves.

Hill Station Retreat: Exploring the Serene Beauty of Araku Valley
  • Hill Station Retreat: Escape to the tranquil surroundings of Araku Valley, nestled amidst the Eastern Ghats, and enjoy its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and tribal culture.
  • Borra Caves: Explore the ancient Borra Caves, known for their stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations, located amidst scenic landscapes in the Ananthagiri Hills.
  • Tribal Museum: Learn about the rich cultural heritage of the indigenous tribes of Andhra Pradesh at the Tribal Museum in Araku, showcasing their art, artifacts, and way of life.
